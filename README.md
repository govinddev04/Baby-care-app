# Baby Care Application
##
Parenting, especially for newborns and infants, comes with numerous challenges. Managing a baby’s feeding, sleep, health, and development can be overwhelming for new parents. Additionally, finding reliable information and keeping track of the baby’s milestones, medical appointments, and supplies like diapers or formula often adds to the stress. 

**The Baby Care Application** provides a comprehensive solution to simplify baby care management and ensure the well-being of the child.

---

## Objective
The objective of this baby care app is to provide parents with a user-friendly platform that helps them efficiently manage their baby’s daily routines and needs. The app integrates features to track feeding, sleep, and growth milestones, offers expert advice on baby health and nutrition, and provides reminders for important tasks. It is designed to support parents in ensuring their baby’s healthy growth and development, making parenting easier and more organized.

---

## Scope, Purpose, Applicability

### **Scope**
This baby care application encompasses a variety of functionalities to meet the needs of parents, including:
- **Personalized Care Tracker:** Log and monitor feeding schedules, diaper changes, and sleep patterns.
- **Developmental Milestone Tracker:** Track baby’s growth and development, with reminders for check-ups and vaccinations.
- **Expert Advice and Resources:** Access articles, tips, and videos from pediatricians and child development specialists.
- **Community Support Features:** Forums or chat options for parents to connect and share experiences.

The system is designed to be scalable and can be enhanced with additional features such as personalized health alerts, nutrition planning tools, and integration with wearable devices.

### **Purpose**
The purpose of this application is to simplify baby care management by offering an all-in-one platform for parents. It helps reduce stress by providing reliable tracking, timely reminders, and expert guidance.

### **Applicability**
- **Parents and Caregivers:** Track feeding, sleep, and milestones.
- **Pediatricians and Health Professionals:** Provide guidance and support.
- **Childcare Centers:** Monitor care and development of children.

---

## Requirement Specification

### **2.1 Functional Requirements**
- **Milestone Tracking Module:** Track developmental milestones based on age and get reminders with activity suggestions.
- **Feeding and Sleep Recommendations:** Personalized recommendations based on age, weight, and feeding history.
- **Health and Wellness Monitoring:** Log vaccinations, growth measurements, and health issues with reminders for check-ups.
- **Expert Advice and Community Support:** Access to expert articles, videos, and forums for parent interactions.

### **2.2 Non-Functional Requirements**
- **Performance:** Support multiple concurrent users during peak times.
- **Reliability and Availability:** 99.9% uptime with data recovery in case of server failures.
- **Scalability:** Able to handle an increasing number of users without performance loss.
- **Compatibility:** Works across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices on multiple browsers/OS.
- **Maintainability:** Modular and well-documented codebase for easy updates and bug fixes.

### **2.3 Hardware Requirements**
- **Device:** Smartphone or basic computer  
- **Operating System:** Android or Windows desktop OS  
- **Processor:** Intel Core i3 or higher  
- **RAM:** 2 GB (smartphones), 4 GB (desktops/laptops)  
- **Internet:** 3G/4G or higher for smooth app usage  

### **2.4 Software Requirements**
- **System:** Windows  
- **Software:** Android Studio, Node.js, Next.js  
- **Back-End:** Node.js
